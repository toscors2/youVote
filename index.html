Voting Begins After All Singers Are Finished Singing and The "Vote Now" Button Appears Above

You Will Have 30 Minutes To Vote

You Will Be Able To Choose Up To 3 Contestants Per Ticket

Each Ticket Can Only Be Used Once

You Can Receive More Tickets From Puchasing Items From The Bar

You Can Also Purchase Tickets for $5 each

Judges Will Figure Their Scores: The Combined Lowest Score (from all 3 judges) From Each Team Will Be Up For Elimination

The Contestant That Is Up For Elimination With The Lowest Audience Votes Will Be Eliminated

In The Event Of A Tie: The Contestant With The Lowest Combined Score (all 3 judges) Will Be Eliminated
